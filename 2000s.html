The 2000s

  • 2001: Apple reveals the Mac OS X operating system, which introduces many new features. Microsoft releases Windows XP, in order to compete with OS X.
  • 2005: Google acquires Android, the Linux-based phone operating system, and creates YouTube.
  • 2006: Apple releases the Macbook Pro, a new dual-core laptop, as well as the Intel-based iMac.
  • 2007: The iPhone is revealed to the world, allowing users to do many things they could previously only do with computers.
  • 2009: Microsoft releases Windows 7, which allows users to pin programs to the taskbar and offers touch and handwriting recognition.
