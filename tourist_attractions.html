Vancouver, BC

Tourist Attractions

  • Vancouver Aquarium

    Visit the Vancouver Aquarium to see over 50,000 creatures and daily shows, or watch the exciting 4D films on display in the theatre!


  • Capilano Suspension Bridge

    Cross the 137 metre long bridge, 70 metres above the Capilano River! Then, walk through the forests with a series of suspension bridges in the Treetops Adventure!


  • Science World

    Discover the wonders of science with hands-on displays, science demonstrations, exhibitions, and immersive films!


  • Vancouver Art Gallery

    A wonderful collection of historical and contemporary art, including artwork by Canadian and Indigenous artists! Don't forget to check out the collection of artworks by Emily Carr!


  • Grouse Mountain

    Take a tram up to the top of Grouse Mountain, and experience the fantastic view, the wildlife refuge, and a host of winter and summertime activities!
