The 1970s

  • 1970: The newly formed company Intel reveals the Intel 1103, the first DRAM chip.
  • 1971: A team of IBM engineers invent the floppy disk, to carry data between computers.
  • 1973: Robert Metcalfe develops Ethernet, to connect computers and other hardware together.
  • 1975: After their success writing software for the Altair 8080 computer, Paul Allen and Bill Gates form Microsoft.
  • 1976: Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak start Apple Computers, and release the Apple I, the first computer with a single-circuit board.
  • 1977: Apple becomes incorporated, and shows off the Apple II, which features colour graphics and an audio cassette drive.
